5 Dimensions of Teaching and Learning

Introduction to the 5D instructional framework

The 5 Dimensions of Teaching and Learning instructional framework combines vision statements and guiding questions to help you reflect on the core elements of effective teaching: purpose, student engagement, curriculum and pedagogy, assessment for student learning, and classroom environment and culture. Shown below are examples on how I incorporate the framework. (Source)

🤝 Building Relationships & Connection

I value relationships and connection in the classroom. When students feel understood, they’re more likely to engage, to trust, and to be open to learning. That starts by really listening and watching how students react during lessons, being responsive when they’re stuck or frustrated, and creating routines where they feel respected and heard.
